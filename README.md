# Market
Implementing a rudimentary market for cryptocurrencies. A class, name "Exchange", has public member functions that allow for the deposit and withdrawl of various assets (e.g. Bitcoin, Etherum and US Dollars). Orders to buy and sell assets at a specified price can also be made and the exchange will perform trades as possible to fulfill orders.
